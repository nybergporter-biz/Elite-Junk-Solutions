Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work? ▾
We price by volume — the more space your junk takes up in our trailer, the more it costs. You can send us photos ahead of time and we'll give you a starting estimate. Final price is always confirmed on-site before we begin. No surprises, no hidden fees.
Do you offer same-day junk removal? ▾
Yes! Same-day service is often available. Call or text us at (801) 441-5090 and we'll let you know our earliest available slot. Online quote requests are typically responded to within 24 hours, so for same-day needs, calling is your fastest option.
What items do you take? ▾
We take the vast majority of household and commercial junk including furniture, appliances, yard waste, exercise equipment, electronics, renovation debris, and much more. We are unable to take hazardous materials like liquid paint, chemicals, fuels, asbestos, medical waste, or propane tanks. See our full list on the Services page.
How is the trailer priced? Do you charge by dimensions? ▾
We price by volume only — not by weight or specific dimensions. Think of it as how much space your items take up in our trailer. A quarter trailer, half trailer, three-quarter trailer, and full trailer are your options. We'll assess on-site and confirm before anything is loaded.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept cash, credit and debit cards, and checks. Payment is collected after the job is completed to your satisfaction.
What areas do you serve? ▾
We are based in South Jordan, Utah and serve the greater Salt Lake County area. Not sure if you're in our service area? Give us a call at (801) 441-5090 and we'll let you know right away.
How should I prepare for my pickup? ▾
To help your pickup go as smoothly as possible:

• Gather all items into one location or pile ahead of time
• Point out exactly which items should be removed
• Clear a path to the items if they're inside
• Keep pets inside during the pickup

That's it — we handle all the heavy lifting!

How It Works

Simple, stress-free junk removal from first contact to final sweep.

1

Call, Text, or Request Online

Start by reaching out however is easiest for you. Call or text (801) 441-5090, or fill out our online quote request form. Include photos of your items if possible — this helps us give you the most accurate starting estimate right away. For same-day service, calling is always the fastest route.

2

Get Your Free Estimate

We'll review your request and provide a clear, upfront starting estimate based on the information and photos you've shared. No vague pricing, no hidden fees — you'll always know what to expect before we show up.

3

Schedule Your Pickup

Choose a day and time that works for your schedule. We offer flexible scheduling Monday through Saturday. Same-day appointments are often available — just ask when you call.

4

We Show Up & Haul It Away

Our team arrives on time within your scheduled window. We confirm the final price on-site before we start (this will match your estimate). Then we do all the heavy lifting — loading your items quickly and carefully. Tip: Have items gathered together and accessible to speed things up.

5

We Sweep Up

Once your junk is loaded up, we don't just leave. We sweep the area so you're left with a truly clean, clear space — not just a room with the stuff removed.

6

Pay & You're Done!

Payment is collected after the job is complete and you're satisfied. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and checks. That's it — your space is clear and you can get on with your day.
